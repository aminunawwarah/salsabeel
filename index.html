About Us

The college was founded by Imam Ridwanullah Jamiu as one of his ingenious endeavors in mentoring young minds regarding the memorization of the Holy Book and also to provide an atmosphere where first school leavers can successfully attain both the Islamic and secular education simultaneously at the expense of neither of them both.
At Salsabeel Islamic School, we offer a 3-year course for primary school graduates to memorize the entire Qur'an in approximately 2 years and engage them in a rigorous revision exercise for 1 year after which a graduation program is conducted to commemorate the success of the newly produced Imams of the Ummah.

We foster a caring culture based on mutual respect, co-operation and support.

Our Vision

Salsabeel Islamic School aims at an epoch-making Islamic center where people can acquire and learn the knowledge of Islam through the mastering and memorization of the Holy Qur'an and traditions of the prophet Muhammad (peace of Allah be upon him); nurturing and encouraging people from all corners of life, strengthening their belief in Allah, developing their innate creativity and inquisitive nature in the pursuance of truth and guidance while anchoring their hearts and souls in a moral framework of a God-centered life.

Our Mission

To prepare Huffaz who would carry the Book of Allah and the sayings of the prophet Muhammad (peace of Allah be upon him) in their hearts, understand them, implement their teachings and share them with others.
